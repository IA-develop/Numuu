ABOUT NUMUU

Numuu.Connect is aiming to bridge the gap between Muslim youth and faith aligned careers through a mentor matching programme. Our fundamental aim is to connect Muslim students to Muslim professionals from various industries to empower students through tailored guidance. Mentoring takes place virtually with a flexible time commitment to fit into differing schedules. For mentors, this offers the chance to give back to the Muslim community in an impactful and time efficient manner. For mentees, this offers the chance to connect with professionals in industries of interest and gain valuable career support and advice.
